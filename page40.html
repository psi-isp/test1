Machine Readable RIAS

Welcome to Machine Readable RIAS!

Regulators are unable to ingest and use RIAS data efficiently to support regulatory analysis as RIAS’s are only published in PDF and HTML formats . Conversion of a subset of RIAS’s will lead to insights and lessons learned, informing an approach for conversion of a large sample.

With Machine Readable RIAS we are 

  1. Structured and consistent RIAS would support retrieval and analysis 
  2. Content and data independent of the RIAS is just as important 
  3. Linking RIAS content/data will support regulatory system wide analysis 

Shape your future web project with sharp design and refine coded functions.

Current Challenges

Poor accessibility of RIAS content and data (e.g. lack of metadata, inconsistent content/data) creates difficulty in analysis of regulations (e.g. linking or comparing RIAS content/data) causing inefficient use of limited resources. The problem is availability of broader regulatory data/content for those who need it for analysis.
