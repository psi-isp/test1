Regulatory Evaluation Platform (REP)

Welcome to the REP Demonstration Project! 

Currently, there is no systematic tool for compiling regulatory information on multiple jurisdictions, by sector/industry, to evaluate complexity, flexibility, conflict, and terminology. The Regulatory Evaluation Platform (REP) presents value in its ability to utilize ML and NLP to analyze structured and unstructured regulatory data, highlight regulations for stock review based on selective criteria and provide a thorough comparative analysis to improve accuracy and efficiency.

Shape your future web project with sharp design and refine coded functions.

How does it work? 

  1. Apply AI and NLP to identify key characteristics (e.g. outdated language, conflicting requirements, harmonization opportunities) within the regulatory stock.
  2. Improve accuracy, speed and productivity when conducting regulatory analysis and stock review.
