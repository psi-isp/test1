What is GC Hubs and Labs? 

.

The Canada School of Public Service (CSPS) initiated the meet-ups in the Fall of 2018 as part of its goal to strengthen capacity across the core public service to use innovative approaches. Alongside its role in training, demonstration projects and research, CSPS is supporting innovation communities of practice across government and with external partners with the view of becoming a gathering place for innovative practices in public administration.
