Behavioural Insights

How can Behavioural Insights help you?

.

Navigating Complex Decisions: Projects that help users to make complicated choices (e.g. choosing saving and investment tools, assessing risk)

Tackling the ‘Last Mile’:  Projects that address issues faced by end users when accessing programs and services
(e.g. uptake)

Addressing an ‘intention-action’ gap: Projects that help users follow through on commitments and plans (e.g. retirement savings, health habits, applying for grants and contributions) 

Improving Compliance: Projects that make it easier or more attractive to follow government processes or rules (e.g. paying taxes, navigating bureaucratic processes)

Shifting Entrenched Habits: Projects that address deeply ingrained habits (e.g. recycling more, hiring and other HR practices) 

BI enhances policies, programs, and regulations that centre on human behaviour. It can also inform the framing and structuring of new policies and programs to reduce behavioural barriers and improve compliance, uptake and impact
