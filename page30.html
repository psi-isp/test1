Behavioural Insights

Behavioural Insights Applied Learning Pilot

.

The Canada School of Public Service partnered with the groundbreaking Behavioural Insights Team, the Policy Community Partnership Office and the Public Service Renewal Secretariat to pilot an applied learning approach to developing public servants’ BI knowledge and skills.

Participating teams:
• Learn about BI principles
• Are coached through the conceptualization of a BI based intervention
• Have access to one-on-one aftercare to support implementation of their intervention

Why is the BI Applied Learning Pilot Different?

Team-Based

Registration is on a team basis to create a critical mass of people to support ongoing work after instruction is complete

Real Problems

Teams do hands-on work to conceptualize a BI intervention for a problem they have identified leading to concrete outcomes and professional development

Aftercare

Once instruction is complete, teams have access to tailored aftercare with BI experts to support implementation and reinforce learnings
