Linkable File Environment

Welcome to the LFE Project




StatsCan’s Linkable File Environment


• Contains complete and up-to-date information on all businesses in Canada based on Statistics         Canada’s Business Register

• Creates the opportunity for new and deeper insights around government and private industry

• Links together administrative and survey data across government

• Reduces the survey and reporting burden of departments and private industry through reusable       and re-linkable data

• Resulting data can be made available for stakeholders and academia


Linkable File Environment Opportunities



Licenses & Registrations Problem
Regulated parties (including individuals) cannot be described without the use of burdensome survey processes.

Expected Outcome
By linking departmental data to Statistics Canada’s administrative and survey data departments can have a more accurate, more comprehensive, and less burdensome understanding of their regulatees. Having a more informed view of regulated industries, firms, and individuals should allow departments to craft more effective program direction and policy. 

Licenses & Registrations Potential insights
Calculate trends in the size, ownership (country of control), location, and
sector of regulated firms.

Better predict economic impact of licensing or registration in terms of revenue growth, employees, exports, research and development, and capital investments of firms, regions, and sectors. 

Licenses & Registrations Current Stage
Initial work with departmental partner underway to better understand regulations
Currently defining scope with Stats Canada including budget and timeframes
Looking to confirm your interest to undertake data assessment and join the project
Ongoing outreach with researchers and academia.
